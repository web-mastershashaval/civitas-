🏛️ CIVITAS — PLATFORM (SYSTEM) RULES

Scope: Platform-wide
Owner: System
Editable by communities: ❌ No
Applies to: Everyone (Members, Facilitators, Co-facilitators, Admins)

1. IDENTITY & ACCOUNT INTEGRITY RULES
SR-1: One Human, One Account

A single person may only control one active account.

Duplicate or detected linked accounts are restricted or suspended.

Enforcement:
→ Account lock or merge review

SR-2: No Impersonation

Users may not impersonate individuals, organizations, or public entities.

Community names and profiles must not falsely represent real entities.

Enforcement:
→ Immediate block + review

SR-3: Authentic Profile Requirement

Users must complete minimum onboarding requirements before interacting:

role selection

community agreement

rules acknowledgment

Enforcement:
→ Action blocked until complete

2. SAFETY & HARM PREVENTION RULES
SR-4: No Violence or Incitement

No content encouraging violence, threats, or physical harm.

Enforcement:
→ Immediate removal + account restriction

SR-5: No Harassment or Targeted Abuse

No persistent harassment, bullying, or intimidation of individuals or groups.

Enforcement:
→ Warning → restriction → suspension (escalating)

SR-6: No Doxxing or Privacy Violations

Sharing private or identifying information without consent is forbidden.

Enforcement:
→ Immediate removal + suspension

SR-7: No Hate-Based Content

No content attacking protected classes at a platform-defined baseline.

Communities cannot relax this rule.

Enforcement:
→ Removal + sanctions

3. DEMOCRATIC FAIRNESS & POWER LIMITS
SR-8: No Silent Moderation

Every moderation action must:

reference a rule

generate a visible reason to the affected user

Enforcement:
→ Action invalid if no reason is logged

SR-9: Facilitators Cannot Edit Member Content

Facilitators may remove or lock content

They may NOT edit, rewrite, or distort member submissions

Enforcement:
→ Action blocked by system

SR-10: No Arbitrary Exclusion

Members cannot be removed, muted, or banned without a rule-based justification.

Enforcement:
→ Action blocked if no rule matches

SR-11: Equal Rule Application

Rules apply equally regardless of:

popularity

tenure

role (except system admins)

Enforcement:
→ System-level validation

4. TRANSPARENCY & AUDITABILITY RULES
SR-12: Mandatory Rule Traceability

Every system decision must log:

rule ID

action taken

timestamp

actor (system or facilitator)

Enforcement:
→ Action fails if logging fails

SR-13: User Right to Explanation

Users must be shown:

why an action was blocked or reversed

which rule was applied

Enforcement:
→ UI-level enforcement

SR-14: Immutable Audit Logs

Moderation and rule logs cannot be deleted or altered.

Enforcement:
→ Database-level immutability

5. RATE LIMITS & ANTI-ABUSE RULES
SR-15: Platform Posting Limits

Global maximums exist for:

posts

comments

reactions

reports

Communities cannot exceed platform caps.

Enforcement:
→ Temporary block with reason

SR-16: No Spam or Flooding

Repetitive, automated, or low-signal content is restricted.

Enforcement:
→ Throttling → temporary lock

SR-17: No Automated Interaction

Bots, scripts, or automation are not allowed in MVP.

Enforcement:
→ Account suspension

6. CONTENT STRUCTURE & ORDER RULES
SR-18: Structured Posting Only

All contributions must:

belong to a board

follow the board’s structure (title, description, etc.)

Enforcement:
→ Submission blocked

SR-19: No Off-Context Posting

Content must match the scope of the board and community.

Enforcement:
→ Removal with explanation

7. GOVERNANCE BOUNDARIES
SR-20: Communities Cannot Override Platform Rules

Any community rule conflicting with system rules is invalid.

Enforcement:
→ Rule rejected at creation

SR-21: Facilitator Power Is Limited

Facilitators cannot:

change platform rules

hide enforcement history

bypass system checks

Enforcement:
→ Action blocked

8. APPEAL & CORRECTION RULES
SR-22: Right to Appeal

Users may appeal moderation actions.

Appeals are logged and time-bound.

Enforcement:
→ Appeal UI always available

SR-23: Appeal Does Not Remove Audit Trail

Even reversed actions remain logged.

Enforcement:
→ Log immutability

9. PLATFORM EVOLUTION RULE
SR-24: Constitution Changes Are Versioned

Platform rules may evolve, but:

changes are versioned

users are notified

old actions keep old rule references

Enforcement:
→ Version control

24 SYSTEM RULES

This is:

Not bloated

Not vague

Fully enforceable

Grant-defensible

MVP-appropriate
